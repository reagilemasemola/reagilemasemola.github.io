ABOUT REAGILE

With perfecting the intricate labyrinth of graphic design, my proficiencies include but are not limited to the Adobe Creative Cloud Software and multiple video and photo editing software. I work well with people and love learning. As a passionate and detail-oriented industry professional, I enjoy approaching complex projects with a holistic approach. A large part of my success while working with people stems from my ability to cultivate an intuitive understanding of their needs, and spearhead the project from there.

Throughout my life, I had been interested in design, which is why I landed with graphic design, a field that covers a large part of the design art and creativity industry. When my friends and family would notice the adverts seen on TV or anywhere else, I would notice the communication design when I went to shopping centers, supermarkets, the high streets of Johannesburg, Pretoria, and other parts of South Africa. Everything we can visually see, is graphic design related.
